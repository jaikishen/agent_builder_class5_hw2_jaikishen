# SkyNova Airlines - Passenger Handbook

*Effective: 1 January 2026 - Version 4.2*

---

## 1. About SkyNova Airlines

SkyNova Airlines is a full-service international carrier headquartered in
Mumbai, India, operating a fleet of Boeing 737-800, Airbus A320neo,
Airbus A350-900, and Boeing 787-9 aircraft. Our network covers eight
international hubs: Delhi (DEL), Mumbai (BOM), London Heathrow (LHR),
New York JFK (JFK), Dubai (DXB), Singapore (SIN), Tokyo Narita (NRT),
and Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG).

This handbook documents the policies that apply to all SkyNova-operated
flights. For codeshare segments operated by partner airlines, the
operating carrier's policy applies.

## 2. Baggage Policy

### 2.1 Carry-on (Cabin) Baggage

Each passenger is permitted **one carry-on bag plus one personal item**.

| Cabin Class       | Carry-on weight | Carry-on dimensions     | Personal item      |
|-------------------|-----------------|--------------------------|--------------------|
| Economy           | 7 kg            | 55 x 35 x 25 cm          | 1 (laptop bag, handbag, small backpack) |
| Premium Economy   | 8 kg            | 55 x 35 x 25 cm          | 1                  |
| Business          | 10 kg total     | 55 x 35 x 25 cm (per piece) | 1               |
| First             | 12 kg total (2 pieces) | 55 x 35 x 25 cm (per piece) | 1          |

Bags exceeding the cabin allowance must be checked in. Gate checking is
free of charge if the bag fits the checked allowance.

### 2.2 Checked Baggage

Free checked baggage allowance per passenger:

| Cabin Class       | Pieces | Max weight per piece | Max linear dimensions |
|-------------------|--------|----------------------|------------------------|
| Economy           | 1      | 23 kg                | 158 cm (L+W+H)         |
| Premium Economy   | 2      | 23 kg                | 158 cm                 |
| Business          | 2      | 32 kg                | 158 cm                 |
| First             | 3      | 32 kg                | 158 cm                 |

Loyalty members receive additional allowance: Silver +1 piece,
Gold +1 piece, Platinum +2 pieces, in addition to fare allowance.

### 2.3 Excess, Overweight, and Oversized Bags

| Condition                                  | Fee (per piece, per segment) |
|--------------------------------------------|------------------------------|
| Extra piece beyond free allowance          | USD 75                        |
| Overweight 23-32 kg (Economy / PE)         | USD 60                        |
| Overweight 32-45 kg (any cabin)            | USD 150                       |
| Oversized 158-203 cm linear                | USD 100                       |
| Sports equipment (golf, ski, surf, bike)   | USD 90                        |
| Musical instruments (oversized)            | USD 90                        |

Bags over 45 kg or 203 cm cannot be accepted as checked baggage and
must be sent as cargo.

### 2.4 Restricted and Prohibited Items

The following items are prohibited in checked baggage and must travel in
cabin or be left behind:

- Lithium-ion batteries above 100 Wh, spare batteries of any size
- Power banks
- Electronic cigarettes and vape devices
- Cash above USD 10,000 equivalent (must be declared)

The following are prohibited in **both** cabin and checked baggage:

- Compressed gases, flammable liquids, oxidisers
- Explosives, fireworks, ammunition (without special permit)
- Wet-cell batteries
- Mercury thermometers and barometers

### 2.5 Lost, Damaged, or Delayed Baggage

Report any baggage issue at the airport baggage desk before leaving the
arrival hall. You will be issued a Property Irregularity Report (PIR)
reference. Compensation under the Montreal Convention applies up to
1,288 SDR (approx USD 1,700) per passenger. If your checked bag is
delayed beyond 24 hours, SkyNova reimburses up to USD 100 for essential
items on production of receipts.

## 3. Cancellation, Changes & Refunds

### 3.1 Twenty-Four Hour Rule

Any booking may be cancelled within **24 hours of issue** for a full
refund, provided departure is at least 7 days away. This applies to all
fare types including non-refundable fares.

### 3.2 Standard Cancellation Fees (after 24 hour window)

| Fare Class            | More than 7 days before departure | 7 days to 24 hours | Less than 24 hours |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------|--------------------|---------------------|
| Economy Saver         | USD 150 fee                        | USD 250 fee         | No refund           |
| Economy Flex          | Free                                | USD 75 fee          | USD 150 fee         |
| Premium Economy       | USD 100 fee                        | USD 200 fee         | USD 350 fee         |
| Business              | Free                                | USD 100 fee         | USD 250 fee         |
| First                 | Free                                | Free                | USD 200 fee         |

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business
days of approval.

### 3.3 Changes

Date and routing changes follow the same fee structure as cancellation,
plus any fare difference. Name changes are not permitted on most fares;
on Business and First the first name change is free, subsequent changes
incur a USD 75 fee.

### 3.4 SkyNova-Initiated Cancellation

If SkyNova cancels a flight, affected passengers are entitled to:

- A full refund to the original payment method, **or**
- Rebooking on the next available SkyNova service in the same cabin,
  **or**
- Rebooking on a partner carrier in the same cabin where space permits

Passengers stranded overnight due to a SkyNova-caused cancellation are
entitled to hotel accommodation, meals, and ground transport at SkyNova
expense. Compensation under the relevant jurisdiction (e.g., EU261 for
flights from EU airports) applies separately.

### 3.5 Delays

Compensation eligibility for departure delays:

- **Under 2 hours:** No compensation; meal vouchers issued if delay
  occurs at the airport during a meal time.
- **2 to 4 hours:** Meal voucher (USD 25) for all passengers; loyalty
  members receive additional miles per their tier.
- **Over 4 hours:** Meal voucher plus rebooking option on next service;
  hotel accommodation if delay extends overnight.
- **Over 6 hours or overnight:** Hotel + meals + ground transport;
  refund or rebooking at passenger choice.

Loyalty miles compensation for 2+ hour delays: Silver 5,000 miles,
Gold 10,000 miles, Platinum 15,000 miles per affected segment.

## 5. Special Assistance and Travel with Pets

### 5.1 Mobility and Accessibility Assistance

Passengers requiring mobility assistance should request service at the
time of booking or at least **48 hours before departure**. Available
codes:

- WCHR - Wheelchair to ramp / can climb stairs unaided
- WCHS - Wheelchair to aircraft door / cannot climb stairs
- WCHC - Wheelchair to seat / immobile passenger

There is no charge for mobility assistance. Personal mobility devices
(folding wheelchairs, mobility scooters) travel free of charge in
addition to the standard baggage allowance.

### 5.2 Visual and Hearing Assistance

Passengers travelling with a service animal (guide dog, hearing
assistance dog) must notify SkyNova at least 48 hours before departure
and present current vaccination and training documentation. Service
animals travel free of charge in cabin without a carrier.

### 5.3 Unaccompanied Minors (UM)

The UM service is available for children aged **5 to 11** years
travelling alone on direct SkyNova flights. The fee is **USD 100 per
segment**. Children 12 to 17 may use the UM service optionally at the
same fee. Below age 5, children must travel with an adult aged 18 or
over.

The pickup contact at destination must present photo ID matching the
name on the UM form. Without ID match, the child will not be released
and will travel back on the next available SkyNova flight at the
parent's expense.

### 5.4 Pet Travel

In-cabin pets are permitted on most routes (some destinations prohibit
pet imports - check before booking). Limits and fees:

- Maximum combined pet + carrier weight: 8 kg
- Carrier dimensions: 43 x 30 x 19 cm soft-sided only
- Fee: USD 125 per segment
- Required documents: vaccination certificate (rabies within 12 months,
  not less than 30 days before travel), health certificate from a
  licensed vet within 10 days of travel
- Confirm at least 48 hours prior; only 2 in-cabin pets per flight

Larger pets travel as checked baggage in the climate-controlled cargo
hold (USD 250 per segment) or as cargo for very large animals. Snub-
nosed breeds (bulldogs, pugs, persian cats) cannot travel as checked or
cargo for safety reasons.

### 5.5 Special Meals

Special meals must be requested at least **24 hours before departure**.
Available codes include: VGML (vegetarian non-dairy), AVML (Asian
vegetarian), HNML (Hindu meal), KSML (Kosher), MOML (Muslim meal),
GFML (gluten free), DBML (diabetic), LCML (low calorie), CHML (child
meal), BBML (baby meal). Allergen-aware preparation can be flagged on
the PNR but SkyNova cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment.

## 6. Boarding and Check-in

### 6.1 Online Check-in

Online check-in opens **24 hours before scheduled departure** and closes
**90 minutes before departure** for international flights, 60 minutes
for domestic. Boarding passes can be downloaded to the SkyNova mobile
app or printed.

### 6.2 Airport Check-in

Counter check-in opens **3 hours before international departure** and
2 hours before domestic departure. Counters close **60 minutes before
departure** for international and 45 minutes for domestic. Late arrival
will result in denied boarding with no refund unless the booking is
in a flexible fare class.

### 6.3 Boarding Process

Boarding begins **40 minutes before departure** in the following order:

1. First and Business class
2. Platinum and Gold tier members (any cabin)
3. Passengers with mobility assistance, families with infants, UMs
4. Premium Economy and Silver tier members
5. Economy zones (rear-to-front by row)

The gate closes **20 minutes before departure**. Passengers not at the
gate at this time forfeit their seat (NoShow status), and the booking
becomes ineligible for refund except where a flexible fare permits it.

### 6.4 Identification

A valid photo ID matching the booking name is required at check-in and
boarding. International travel requires a passport valid for at least
6 months from the date of return travel. SkyNova is not responsible for
denied boarding due to invalid travel documents.

## 7. In-flight Services

### 7.1 Meals

Meals are complimentary on all flights over 2 hours. Short-haul flights
(under 2 hours) offer a complimentary snack and beverage; additional
items available for purchase. Premium cabins receive multi-course meals
on flights over 4 hours.

### 7.2 In-flight Entertainment (IFE)

Wide-body aircraft (A350, B787) feature seat-back IFE with a library of
over 1,000 movies, TV shows, music albums, and games. Narrow-body
aircraft (B737, A320) offer streaming IFE to personal devices over the
on-board network - please bring headphones and a charged device.

### 7.3 Wifi

In-flight wifi is available on all wide-body aircraft. Pricing:

- Messaging only (whatsapp, sms): Free for all passengers
- Browsing pass (1 hour): USD 9
- Full-flight pass: USD 19
- Free for Platinum and Business class passengers; complimentary 1-hour
  pass for Gold tier

If wifi fails to connect for the duration of your flight, the fee is
fully refundable on request through customer service.

### 7.4 Power and Connectivity

USB-A and USB-C ports are available at every seat on wide-body aircraft.
Universal power outlets are available in Premium Economy and above on
wide-bodies. Narrow-body aircraft have USB-A only at most rows.
